# PrivateAI - 3 Minute Presentation Script

Target length: about 3 minutes spoken. Keep demo clips very short, or talk over the first few seconds.

## Slide 1 - Title, 8 seconds

Hi, I am [Your Name], and this is PrivateAI: one click to deploy your own private AI server. No cloud expertise. No privacy trade-offs.

## Slide 2 - Hook, 13 seconds

Quick question: have you ever had an AI conversation you would not want shared? Medical advice, legal questions, client data, company code. With public AI tools, those conversations are not truly private.

## Slide 3 - The Problem, 15 seconds

Today, using AI often means surrendering your privacy. Prompts can be logged, stored, reviewed, leaked, or subpoenaed. For doctors, lawyers, journalists, and engineers, that is a dealbreaker.

## Slide 4 - The Alternative, 15 seconds

The alternative is self-hosting, and that is painful. You need cloud setup, GPU drivers, SSH keys, firewall rules, model installs, and security configuration. It takes days, and one mistake can expose everything.

## Slide 5 - Vision, 10 seconds

Our vision is simple: anyone should be able to use AI privately. Not just developers. Not just enterprises. Every professional should get private AI without becoming a cloud expert.

## Slide 6 - Solution, 15 seconds

PrivateAI gives you the best of both worlds. We provision a private AI server inside your own cloud account. Your data never touches our servers. You get one-click setup, hardware-level encryption, and real-time cost control.

## Slide 7 - How It Works, 15 seconds

Here is the flow. Choose your cloud and GPU profile, including confidential H100 options with AMD SEV-SNP. Connect credentials or OAuth. PrivateAI handles provisioning, drivers, firewall, models, and the dashboard.

## Slide 8 - User Story, 15 seconds

Think about Sarah, a nurse whose hospital blocks public AI because patient data cannot leave the network. With PrivateAI, she deploys on the hospital's Azure subscription. Compliance is satisfied, and she gets hours back.

## Slide 9 - Demo 1, 13 seconds

This is the deployment flow. From the dashboard, we create a deployment, select a confidential GPU profile, and start provisioning. The dashboard streams all 13 setup steps in real time.

## Slide 10 - Demo 2, 13 seconds

Once ready, the user clicks Connect and Chat. Open WebUI runs against their own VM. Prompts are encrypted in transit, never stored by PrivateAI, and never used for training.

## Slide 11 - Demo 3, 13 seconds

The dashboard gives full control. Users monitor instances, start and stop VMs, connect to each server, and set budget auto-shutdown so private AI does not become a surprise cloud bill.

## Slide 12 - Impact, 15 seconds

This unlocks AI for the people who need privacy most: legal teams, healthcare teams, journalists, and engineers working with proprietary code. PrivateAI makes safe AI practical.

## Slide 13 - Team, 8 seconds

We built this because the choice between useful AI and private AI should not exist. PrivateAI is simple enough for normal users, and secure enough for serious work.

## Slide 14 - Closing, 5 seconds

With PrivateAI, it is your data, your models, and your conversations. Finally, actually private. Thank you.
